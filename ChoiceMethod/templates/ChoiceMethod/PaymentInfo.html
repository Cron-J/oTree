Your payment for this task depends on your decision. You will be presented with {{Constants.num_decisions}} decisions where each time you have to decide whether you are willing to pay the stated amount for a certain product or not. At the end of the task, one of the decisions will be chosen at random. If for the stated amount in that decision you decided to click "yes", you will have bought the product for this price. In this case you will receive the product (sent by post) plus the rest of your endowment. If you clicked "no" you will receive your full endowment of {{Constants.endowment}}.
