In this task you will be grouped with 2 other participants. Therefore, the group consists of three members: A (you), B and C. Each of you will go through three consecutive steps:

  1. Everyone in your group will be asked how much he or she is willing to pay for a certain sustainable product.
  2. Then your willingness to pay will be communicated to group member B, and you will learn the willingness to pay of C.
  3. Finally, you will be asked to predict the willingness to pay of B.

So, what do you know when you predict the willingness to pay for B in Step 3? Your own willingness to pay and that of C.
